About Us

Sinergi Insan Andalan serves as a bridge between non-formal education and industry needs. We understand that one of the biggest challenges for graduates and job seekers is not just acquiring theoretical knowledge but also developing practical, job-ready skills that meet industry demands.

With a hands-on, industry-driven, and results-oriented approach, we offer training programs, bootcamps, and certifications designed to equip individuals with the skills that today’s companies are actively looking for. We believe that learning is not just about theory—it’s about real-world application and meaningful experiences that prepare individuals for the workforce.
